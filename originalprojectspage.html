Temperature Converter for iOS

One of my most recent personal projects has included developing a temperature conversion app for iOS (my app is pictured above). Using Storyboards and Swift in XCode, it implements a temperature slider that the user can use to pick a temperature which will then have its corresponding Celcius and Fahrenheit temperatures displayed in real-time on the screen.
