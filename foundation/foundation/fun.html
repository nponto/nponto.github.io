During the Summers, I enjoy taking trips up North around Minnesota to see different parks and landmarks. One of my favorites has been splitrock lighthouse in Duluth (pictured above). The Twin Cities is a pretty flat area so it's fun to go to Duluth and see some cliffs and waterfalls. I've taken a few trips up here and will usually go to my friend's cabin that is right on Superior. We usually do a lot of hiking and visit most of the popular state parks up there.


One of my favorite memories in Duluth is when we went to Illgen Falls and did some cliff jumping. It was somewhat dangerous to do, but luckily we had some well-experienced locals to give us some tips on where to jump. We did this at a few spots, including multiple at Illgen, but this was by far the highest jump (about 50 feet)


Lastly, one of my favorite hobbies is fishing. I spend a lot of time doing this in the Summer while I'm up North volunteering at my Summer Camp and when I'm just vacationing. My favorite spot to fish is the private lake at my Summer Camp. We'll usually wake up around 5:00am, hike to the lake, then fish on the rowboat until the sun is up.
